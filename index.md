## ✨ About Bloomly

Bloomly is a personal finance app designed with mindful aesthetics and uncompromising privacy. Track your transactions, set savings goals, and watch your finances bloom — all while keeping your data 100% private.

### What makes Bloomly different?

- 🌷 **4 beautiful themes** — Rose, Lavender, Sage, Peach
- 💰 **One-time purchase** — Lifetime access, no subscriptions
- 🚫 **No ads, no tracking, no servers** — ever
- 🔒 **Privacy-first** — Your data NEVER leaves your iCloud
- ☁️ **iCloud sync** across all your Apple devices
- 📱 **Home Screen Widgets** in 3 sizes (Small, Medium, Large)
- 🌍 **12 languages supported**

### Supported Languages

English, German, Spanish (Spain & Mexico), French (France & Canada), Italian, Polish, Portuguese (Brazil), Japanese, Turkish, Croatian.

### Privacy Promise

> **We don't have your data. We don't want it. Your finances live in YOUR iCloud, encrypted by Apple. We see nothing.**

That's it. That's the whole policy. 🌸
