## ✨ About Bloomly

Bloomly is a personal finance app designed with feminine aesthetics and uncompromising privacy. Track your transactions, set savings goals, and watch your finances bloom — all while keeping your data 100% private.

### What makes Bloomly different?

- 🌷 **4 beautiful themes** — Rose, Lavender, Sage, Peach
- 💰 **One-time purchase** — Lifetime access, no subscriptions required
- 🔒 **Privacy-first** — Your data NEVER leaves your iCloud
- ☁️ **Syncs across Apple devices** — iPhone, iPad, Mac
- 🌍 **11 languages supported**
- 🚫 **Zero ads, zero tracking, zero servers**

### Privacy Promise

> **We don't have your data. We don't want it. Your finances live in YOUR iCloud, encrypted by Apple. We see nothing.**

That's it. That's the whole policy. 🌸
