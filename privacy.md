# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 8, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Bloomly ("Bloomly," "we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application "Budget Planner - Bloomly" (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy by following a privacy-first approach: **your financial data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account — never on our servers.**

Bloomly is developed by Sasa Stevic, an independent developer based in Germany.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

Unlike most finance apps, Bloomly does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit:

- ❌ Your transactions, income, or expense data
- ❌ Your savings goals or budget information
- ❌ Your account names or balances
- ❌ Your personal name (if entered in app settings)
- ❌ Your email address
- ❌ Your phone number
- ❌ Your contacts
- ❌ Your photos or files
- ❌ Your location
- ❌ Your browsing history
- ❌ Device identifiers for advertising purposes

**We have no servers. We have no database. We never see your financial information.**

## How Your Data Is Stored

All your data is stored in two places, both controlled entirely by **YOU**:

### 1. On Your Device

Your transactions, categories, savings goals, and preferences are stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

### 2. In Your Personal iCloud Account

If you have iCloud enabled and signed in, your data is automatically and **end-to-end encrypted** by Apple, then synced across your Apple devices via Apple's CloudKit service. This is the same secure technology Apple uses for iCloud Photos and iCloud Drive.

**Important:** This data lives in **YOUR** iCloud account — not ours. We have no access to it. Apple is the data processor; you are the data controller.

To manage your iCloud sync:

- Go to **iPhone Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Apps Using iCloud → Bloomly**
- Toggle on/off as you wish

## Home Screen Widgets

Bloomly includes Home Screen widgets in three sizes (Small, Medium, Large). The widget functionality:

- Reads data **only** from the App Group container shared between the app and the widget extension
- All data remains on your device — widgets do not transmit any information
- Widget snapshots are stored as local JSON files in your device's protected sandbox
- Tap actions open the main app via custom URL scheme (`bloomly://`)

**Widgets do not transmit any data, access the network, or send analytics.**

## Third-Party Services We Use

Bloomly uses a limited number of third-party services for essential functionality:

### RevenueCat (Purchase Management)

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. RevenueCat receives:

- An **anonymous user ID** (random string, not linked to your identity)
- Your **purchase status** (e.g., "Lifetime Pro: active")
- The **product purchased** (e.g., `bp_lifetime_1299`)
- Your **country** (for tax calculations, anonymized)

RevenueCat does **NOT** receive your name, email, financial data, or any personal information. They process payments and verify entitlements only.

RevenueCat's Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### Apple App Store / In-App Purchases

When you make a purchase, Apple handles the payment through your existing Apple ID. Apple is the merchant of record. Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

### Apple iCloud (CloudKit)

As described above, your data syncs through Apple's iCloud service. Apple's privacy practices are described here: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## Data We Do Not Share

Because we do not collect your data, we cannot:

- Sell it to advertisers
- Share it with marketing companies
- Provide it to analytics platforms
- Disclose it to data brokers

**No third party will ever see your transactions, balances, or financial situation through Bloomly.**

## Analytics and Tracking

Bloomly contains **NO analytics tracking**:

- ❌ No Google Analytics
- ❌ No Facebook SDK
- ❌ No Mixpanel
- ❌ No Amplitude
- ❌ No third-party advertising trackers
- ❌ No device fingerprinting
- ❌ No App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompts needed

The app does not connect to any analytics services. We do not know how many users we have, what features they use, or how long they spend in the app.

## Notifications

Bloomly uses local notifications for:

- Daily expense reminders
- Goal milestone celebrations (50%, 75%, 100%)
- Recurring transaction reminders

**All notifications are 100% local.** No data is transmitted to any server. Notifications are handled entirely by Apple's UserNotifications framework on your device. We do not use APNs (Apple Push Notification Service) or any remote push system.

You can disable notifications at any time:

- In-app: **Profile → Notifications**
- iOS Settings: **Settings → Notifications → Bloomly**

## Children's Privacy

Bloomly is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. The app is designed for adults managing personal finances.

## Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, and Beyond)

Because we don't collect your data, **most data privacy rights don't directly apply to us** — there's no data to access, delete, or transfer from our systems. However:

- **Right to Access:** All your data is visible to you directly in the app at any time
- **Right to Delete:** Delete the app from your device and disable iCloud sync to permanently remove all data
- **Right to Portability:** Use the "Export to CSV" feature in Profile to download all your transactions as a standard CSV file (Pro feature)
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Disable iCloud sync in iPhone Settings at any time

For data stored in **YOUR** iCloud, Apple is the data controller. Contact Apple directly for iCloud-specific privacy requests.

### Special Note for EU Users (GDPR)

As an EU-based developer (Germany), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). However, because Bloomly is designed as a zero-data-collection app:

- **No personal data is processed** by Bloomly or its developers
- **No consent is required** because no data leaves your devices
- **Data Protection Officer (DPO):** Not applicable (we process no personal data)
- **Lawful basis for processing:** Not applicable (no processing occurs)

If you have GDPR-related questions, you may contact us at the email below.

## Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as:

- You keep the app installed on your device, AND
- You keep your iCloud account active with sync enabled

When you delete the app and disable iCloud sync, all your Bloomly data is permanently deleted from your iCloud after Apple's standard deletion process (typically immediate).

## In-App Purchase Information

When you purchase Pro (Lifetime $12.99):

1. Apple processes the payment through your Apple ID
2. Apple sends a purchase receipt to RevenueCat
3. RevenueCat marks your anonymous user ID as "Pro: active"
4. Your purchase status is checked locally when you open the app

**No payment information** (credit card numbers, bank details, billing addresses) ever passes through Bloomly or its developers.

## Face ID and Biometric Authentication

If you enable Face ID Lock in Profile settings:

- Face ID data is **never** sent to Bloomly or any server
- Face ID is handled entirely by Apple's secure enclave on your device
- We only receive a "success/failure" signal from iOS
- Disable anytime in Profile → Face ID Lock
